Sarah Kenney

School: Trinity College

I study at Trinity College majoring in Psychology, and minoring in Rhetoric, Writing & Media Studies. I thrive on staying busy – balancing academics, athletics, and everything in between. Being part of the Trinity Women’s Varsity Soccer team has taught me more than just how to compete; it’s helped me strengthen my leadership skills, manage time efficiently, and stay grounded under pressure.

This year, I was inducted into Trinity’s chapter of Chi Alpha Sigma, the National Scholar-Athlete Society, and I’ve consistently made faculty honors while playing a sport I love.

In the Spring of 2024, I was honored to represent my peers as the sole female athlete speaking at the dedication ceremony for our new athletic facilities – a moment that meant a lot to me.

Outside of class and soccer, I enjoy staying active through running, yoga, and skiing, but I also love slowing down and spending time with my friends, or exploring new places. Whether I’m on the field or in the classroom, I care deeply about giving my best effort and living my life with integrity.
